Incorrect? See Why Below

Our project is not perfect, and often makes mistakes and may give incorrect labels if a berry is poisonous. This is due to small mistakes in the early stages of our model’s development, such as pixelated images, labeling errors, and not having enough data to ensure the model is correct.

Our project could have been improved if we had more time, which we might have used to come up with new types of categories to simplify the model’s task, being able to pick out more high resolution images, and making sure that our labels were extremely consistent.
